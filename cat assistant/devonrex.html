Devon Rex

Description

The Devon Rex is distinguished by its triangular face, enormous, low-set ears, and large eyes, which give it an alien look. The Devon Rex has a thin, wavy coat that is available in a variety of hues and patterns in addition to its remarkable facial traits. The Cat Fanciers' Association officially recognised the breed in 1979, and in 1983 it was given championship status. The Devon Rex is very social, sociable, and active. It complements any family structure and is a fantastic playmate due to its athletic character. The Devon Rex is prone to a few illnesses, but with routine checkups at the doctor, your cat should live a long, healthy life. This adorable, mischievous kitty is a real treasure. They weigh 2.72 to 4 kg.

Behavior

The Devon Rex is a tiny but formidable cat with a cheerful disposition. These cats communicate by their lively personalities and active activity, even if they are not as noisy as other breeds. Devon Rexes enjoy cuddling with their family's pets as well as people.

Lifespan

9 to 15 years

Characteristics

Because of its unusual, almost alien appearance, the Devon Rex is well known. They feature broad cheekbones, big eyes, big noses, and low-set, rounded ears. Small fur tufts can occasionally be seen on the ears. Devon Rexes have long, athletic legs, huge toes, and slender, muscular bodies.
