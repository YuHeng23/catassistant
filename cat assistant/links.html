Online Resources

The Cat Fanciers' Association

In the United States, the Cat Fanciers' Association (CFA) was founded in 1906. The CFA presently maintains the largest database of purebred cats in existence. The association's declared goals are to preserve and advance purebred cat breeds while simultaneously improving the welfare of all cats. Along with its well-established operation in the United States and Canada, the association presently has a recognised presence in Europe, China, and Japan. Their website provides many detailed information about breeds that are certified by this organization.

Aside from promoting their association, it also provides various breeding services that users can set the breeds they prefer and the location the user is in, information about the breeds that are recognized by the association that are eligible for the household pet class, and also advice about caring about cats and building a cattery, which are useful for cat owners.

Visit their website

CatHealth.com

Cathealth.com is a website that focuses on the health symptoms, cat care, behavior, grooming, breed characteristics, and tips in the form of articles. Furthermore, it also provides a live chat section where users can ask a cat veterinarian about the specific problems they face so they don't have the need to search for another veterinarian to contact. Information is given in the form similar to a Google search, which provides a list of articles related to the problem that is entered.

Visit their website

WebMD

The website for pets maintained by WebMD, a company that offers health information, focuses on offering resources for both cats and dogs in terms of healthcare. Numerous health-related topics, including cat behaviour, diet and nutrition, basic cat care, and common health issues are covered in the website's section on cats. It offers knowledge in a variety of formats, including blogs, slideshows, quizzes, and videos. In addition, it covers a wide range of topics, including common health issues, understanding your pets, diet, and nutrition.

Visit their website

Facebook Groups

Malaysia Cat Community

Malaysia Cat Community (MCC) is a club that aims to unite all animal lovers especially cats and this club is a non-profit organization. The group makes new posts to welcome new members who joins their community, and is active with posts related to their pet cats and events such as cat shows and cat adoptions.

Visit their group.

Cat for Adoption & Education Malaysia Official

This Facebook group is active with members posting their cats that they are willing to let go, allowing other members that are interested to adopt them. Details of their cats are mentioned in their posts so that membersd will know what they are adopting. Members also post their question about their pet cat's behaviours and answers are posted in the comments actively. Furthermore, products related to cats are also advertised with a fee that will be proceeded to the organization that made this group's stray cat shelter.

Visit their group.

Subreddits

r/CatAdvice

The cat advice and support subreddit is a community made by and for cat enthusiasts. Everyone is welcomed into their community regardless of background, experience, or opinion. There are a variety of flairs that filter their posts based on what the user needs. For example, a New to Cats/Just Adopted flair exists so that users with similiar experience can ask questions and other experienced users can answer them. It is an extremely helpful community for every cat owners. They also host events such as allowing users to ask a certified cat trainer and behaviour consultant.

Visit subreddit.

r/AskVet

This subreddit is a community in which current or prospective animal animal owners can ask questions and get advice from professionals from the veterinary community. However, it still should be treated as a secondary source of information as diagnosis should be done physically instead of relying on online posts.

Visit subreddit.
